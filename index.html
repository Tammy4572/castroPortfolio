About Me

I graduated from Trident Technical College, where I earned an Associates Degree in Computer Programming. I am also a graduate of The Iron Yard, an intensive 12-week coding bootcamp in Charleston, SC, that focused primarily on front-end coding using React and Redux.

The foundation of my professional experience includes web development, graphic design, marketing, project management, and IT support. I managed and provided web content and marketing solutions including system design, company branding, and social media and graphic design for a non-profit in Summerville, SC. I implemented a customized Salesforce CRM solution, trained all staff on its use, and created PDF- and video-based tutorials to support future employees and volunteers.
